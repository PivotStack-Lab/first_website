Day Four: Broke

How I got here

It had all come to a point. I was crystal clear and needed change. and the change was to go back, not forward. I remembered playing with my dad's computer when I was 12 years old. The World Wide Web was celebrating it's first birthday. I was trying to parition the hard drive. I succeeded!

I realised that I had the confidence, mindset and skills to now have a strong chance of success at making a career in the tech sector. So I started to use those skills and researched the best ways to get a free education. I found The Odin Project.

I was feeling confident. I had done this kind of thing before. I knew HTML boilerplate, how hard could it be!? I had installed a virtual machine to run linux inside my windows machine. It was clunky, and I was feeling good, so I thought 'why not partition the hard drive? Dual booting worked for me before and it'll work for me again.

Am I the drama?

The Odin Project website made it sound pretty simple. And it was at first. Make some space on the drive. Make a flash drive. Reboot the computer and boot from it. It did not work out that way. I couldn't get windows to make space on the drive, according to Google this was due to it storing a number of hidden files on there. All I had to do was download this other software, use it to make space and bingo.

Following the steps I come to a screen showing my laptop booting into repair mode. Oh dear. I watch as it starts to cycle and reboot itself. Over and over again. Can Gemini help me? I ask it to give me some ideas, and it has a few! We're back in business.
